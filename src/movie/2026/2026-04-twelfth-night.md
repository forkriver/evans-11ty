Assiniboine Theatre Company and Brandon University Drama present *Twelfth Night*, Shakespeare's fast-paced comedy full of romance, mistaken identities and practical jokes.

## Venue

**Note:** This play takes place at the Westman Centennial Auditorium, *not* in the Evans Theatre.

## Tickets

Tickets for *Twelfth Night* cost $16.00–$30.00 and are available from the [<abbr title="Westman Centennial Auditorium">WMCA</abbr> box office](https://www.wmca.ca/events/twelfth-night-assiniboine-company-and-bu-drama-department).
