If you're trying to find the perfect gift for That Special Someone, or just That Person Who's Hard to Shop For, consider getting them an Evans Theatre 5-Movie Pass!

Pros:
- 5 movies!
- Discounted admission price!
- Transferable!
- Never expires!

Cons:
- uh
- none that I can think of

Our passes are great for birthdays, holiday gift season, anniversaries, or Just Because!

To buy a 5-Movie Pass:
- Come to the Box Office before [any regularly-scheduled movie](/upcoming-movies/)
- Drop us a line at [info@evanstheatre.ca](mailto:info@evanstheatre.ca?subject=Evans%205-Movie%20Pass)

See you at the movies!

*Fine print:* <small>Our 5-Movie Pass is accepted for admission at any regular Evans weekend movie presented by the volunteers of the Brandon Film Festival, Inc. Special events at the Evans may not accept them.</small>
